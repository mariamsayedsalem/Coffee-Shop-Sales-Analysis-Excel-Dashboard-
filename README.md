## 📖 Project Overview
This project analyzes over **149,000 transactions** from a coffee shop chain across three locations in New York City (Astoria, Hell's Kitchen, and Lower Manhattan). The goal was to transform raw transactional data into a dynamic **Excel Dashboard** to track performance and identify sales trends.

---

## 🎯 Key Objectives
- 🧹 **Data Cleaning:** Processed and structured a large dataset (149k+ rows).
- 🔄 **Feature Engineering:** Created calculated columns for Revenue, Hour, Day, and Month for deeper analysis.
- 📈 **Interactive Visualization:** Developed a dynamic dashboard using Pivot Tables and Slicers for real-time data filtering.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **Microsoft Excel:** Advanced Formulas, Pivot Tables, and Data Modeling.
- **Data Visualization:** Interactive Charts and Slicers.

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- ✅ **Total Revenue:** Generated over **$232K** in sales.
- ✅ **Total Transactions:** Successfully analyzed **49K+** customer orders.
- ✅ **Peak Performance:** Identified peak hours (**7:00 AM - 10:00 AM**) for optimal staffing.
- ✅ **Top Category:** Confirmed **Coffee** as the primary revenue driver.

---

## 💡 Business Insights
- **Sales Growth:** A consistent upward trend in revenue was observed from January through June.
- **Location Analysis:** Lower Manhattan emerged as the top-performing location.
- **Product Mix:** "Large" sized beverages contribute significantly more to the total revenue compared to other sizes.
